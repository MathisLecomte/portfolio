Biography

My name is Mathis LECOMTE. Born in Paris, I grew up in a small suburb of the inner suburbs.
I am currently at university, in second year of DUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Profession), and I finish this by looking for an internship in the different areas that I tackled during my training.
This mainly includes mobile (Android, Flutter) and web (customer-oriented and server), database management (MySQL and NoSQL), Game Design (Unity), creation audiovisual, and finally Motion Design.

My philosophy is related to the use of each new notion within my different projects, in order to improve existing functionalities and find new avenues of research. Curious and versatile, I learn very quickly from what I observe, and I enjoy creating, with support from my Hobbies.

During my free time, I am passionate about robotics, Street Art, music, and literature. I compose songs of Jazz, of Neo-Soul, work on the construction of a robot Jerry and regularly make improvements to my desktop and mobile software in development.

In short, my fields of activity are multiple because I am curious to learn, and seek to understand what I observe.
