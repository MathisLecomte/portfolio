The website

February 15, 2021

The present site on which you are browsing does not inherit any template (except Google fonts / icons). The animations, the stylesheet and the layout were "handmade".
The aim of this website is to be able to clearly present who I am, what I do and how I the fact. The challenge might seem simple, but it was complex.
Indeed, the key is not to list what we are, but to think about how to synthesize and enhance it.

So, I decided to combine my skills in three sections:

  • Development ("dev" for friends),
  • VFX (special effects, motion design),
  • and game design (conception of 3D models, programming on Unity).

In this article, we are going to see how the ergonomics of this portfolio have been thought out.

Style sheet

Let's start with the stylesheet, the most visible element on any web page. Per sheet of style, I hear the colors, the shapes of the widgets, the animations and transtitions, without forgetting the typography.

Couleurs

Colors

First of all, I decided to place a dark background, because this is the environment that I choose to work, to code, to think. Then, the accent color had to stand out sufficiently from the background color, without assault. In addition, she had to correspond to me, without copying some of my important projects with their own color code. Finally, the color must remain relatively neutral in relation to the theme that the website addresses: the portfolio.

--accent: rgb(0, 102, 204);

Typography

The typography had to correspond to the three areas covered in this portfolio, without neglecting one or the other. A fixed-width typography would enhance the field of programming by relaxing the VFX, while that a typography with serif would have lost the game design and the programming.

font-family: 'Audiowide', cursive;

Animations / transitions

Animations and transitions are used to create benchmarks and facilitate understanding of navigation within the application or website.
These animations were all the more useful since the contents of the different tabs are contained in the home page.

Navigation

The navigation within the site is intended to be simple. It is possible, by a simple scroll, to navigate between all the sections, and thus to have an overview of the projects, carefully classified.
This technique is immersive.
